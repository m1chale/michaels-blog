How I collaborate with others

12.12.2022

Imagine you are responsible for customer service at a technology company. Fortunately, your company enjoys high customer satisfaction. Until one day it plummets. It turns out you've received a wave of complaints about a new software update. This is the first you've heard of such an update. You investigate and find that the various departments just aren't talking to each other. The IT department failed to inform you and the marketing department, so customers were neither informed nor warned. Worse, they failed to ask customers for feedback on what the update should include, and now they can't access some of the features they liked best.
